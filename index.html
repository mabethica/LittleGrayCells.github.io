Parker Pyne Investigates

Agatha Christie - Book

The past few weeks I have been picking up Parker Pyne Investigates to have a read. The joy of the story is the ease in which you can pick it up for a short read of one of his exploits, put it down again, and not feel you are losing from the experience.

Unlike many of her characters Mr Parker Pyne only has one book with many short stories within it, I do find this slightly dissappointing as I enjoyed the concept of his character: a man who can fix your unhappiness.
